Guess the secret word

You can guess a word or a short phrase. The secret word can be any length, but will be a single word (and not a phrase).

The game will tell how semantically close the word is to the secret word. Kinship is not expressed in spelling but in meaning. Two words are semantically closer the more likely they are to be used in a similar context. 100 This is the secret word itself.

The secret word can be any part of speech, but will always be a single word.

The game will tell you how close your guess is to the secret word and indicate if the word is one of the 1000 words closest to the secret word. (The 1000 close words received today by players.)

Unlike Wordle, you usually need more than 6 guesses. More like a few dozen. And there is a new word every day. The word is renewed at 1 am.
